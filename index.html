DENT A.R.

The primary objective of this platform is to create engaging Augmented Reality (AR) models that children can easily access and interact with through their mobile devices. By leveraging AR technology, we aim to present STEM concepts in a fun, immersive, and highly visual manner, thereby fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for these fields in young minds.
